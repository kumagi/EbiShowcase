# Ebi Strike generated art

These original raster assets were generated for Ebi Showcase with the built-in
OpenAI image generation tool. They avoid third-party characters, brands and
existing game franchises and are distributed under the repository's Apache
License 2.0.

## `assets/strike-pearl-coliseum.png`

Prompt:

> Create an original underwater pearl-reef coliseum for a contemporary premium
> mobile drag-and-launch game. Use a high three-quarter top-down portrait 2:3
> view with an open oval arena of iridescent shellstone and shallow luminous
> water, gold trajectory inlays, pearl arches and coral gardens around the
> edges, and deep ocean beyond. Keep the central board clear for moving pieces.
> Environment only; no characters, enemies, obstacles, UI, text, logos,
> watermark or copied locations.

Generated at 1024x1536 RGB and optimized to 720x1080 RGB PNG.

## `assets/strike-allies-atlas.png`

Prompt:

> and Momo, a rosy axolotl tide mage curled around a luminous pearl orb. Use
> premium contemporary mobile-game rendering, high three-quarter top-down view
> and circular impact silhouettes on a perfectly uniform #ff00ff chroma-key
> background. No shadows, floor, overlap, text, logos, watermark or copied

Generated at 2048x1024 RGB, chroma-keyed with the imagegen skill's soft matte
and despill helper, then optimized to a 1024x512 RGBA two-cell atlas.
