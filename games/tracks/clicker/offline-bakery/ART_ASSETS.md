# Offline Bakery generated art

These original raster assets were generated for Ebi Showcase with the built-in
OpenAI image generation tool. They deliberately avoid third-party characters,
brands and existing game franchises, and are distributed under the repository's
Apache License 2.0.

## `assets/bakery-pearl-palace.png`

Prompt:

> Create an original luxurious underwater patisserie interior carved into a
> pearl-and-brass reef palace for a contemporary premium mobile clicker game.
> Grand arched aquarium windows show manta silhouettes and coral gardens;
> opalescent counters, warm pastry cases, pearl lamps, brass pipes, magical
> ovens and layered shelves frame an open center. Use a polished hand-painted
> portrait 2:3 composition with warm amber bakery light against luminous aqua
> ocean. Background only; no characters, UI, text, logos, watermark or copied
> location.

Generated at 1024x1536 RGB and optimized to 720x1080 RGB PNG.

## `assets/bakery-chef.png`

Prompt:

> Create exactly one original friendly anthropomorphic coral-orange shrimp
> master pastry chef and shopkeeper, full-body in three-quarter pose, with teal
> eyes, curled antennae, pearl-white chef coat, navy apron and cream chef hat.
> The chef presents a luminous shell-shaped coral pastry on a gold tray and
> makes a welcoming gesture. Use highly polished contemporary mobile-game
> character rendering on a perfectly uniform #ff00ff chroma-key background;
> no shadow, floor, text, logo, watermark, extra character or franchise copy.

Generated at 1024x1536 RGB, chroma-keyed with the imagegen skill's soft matte
and despill helper, then optimized to 512x768 RGBA PNG.

## `assets/bakery-equipment-atlas.png`

Prompt:

> Create exactly three isolated underwater patisserie production machines in
> equal cells of a wide 3:1 atlas: a pearl-shell convection oven with a coral
> pastry inside, a brass-and-aqua enchanted stand mixer with luminous batter,
> and an elegant miniature pastry boutique kiosk. Match the same premium
> hand-painted three-quarter mobile-game style and scale. Use a perfectly
> uniform #ff00ff chroma-key background with clear cell gaps; no shadows,
> floor, text, numbers, logos, watermark or copied designs.

Generated at 2048x683 RGB, chroma-keyed with a soft matte and despill, then
optimized to a 1536x512 RGBA atlas. The three exact cells are embedded once and
used for in-world production lines and purchase cards.
