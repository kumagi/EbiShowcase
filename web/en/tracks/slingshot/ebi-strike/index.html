DEEP DIVE / CONTACT PIPELINE

Turn the same “bump” into events for each target.

Every moving frame checks walls, rocks, enemies, and allies in order. Push overlapping circles apart and reflect first; then a guard receives direct damage while an ally sends an area wave. It remains one turn until motion stops.

Keep aiming separate from motion

Physics and contacts run only while moving is true. Switch turns at the exact stop frame, then accept the next drag.

active.pos = active.pos.add(active.velocity)
bounceWalls(&active)
hitEnemies(&active)
hitAlly(&active)
active.velocity = active.velocity.scale(0.982)

WHY COOLDOWN?

Count one contact once

A short timer on each enemy prevents a circle overlapping for several frames from removing all HP at once.

WHY ONE ALLY WAVE?

Keep each shot meaningful

One wave per shot prevents two allies bouncing together from creating endless free attacks.
