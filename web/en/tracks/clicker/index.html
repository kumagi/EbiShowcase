Idle Clicker

Build an idle clicker like the phone games where a button tap makes numbers go up on their own. Grow one click into automatic production, prices that rise on a set schedule (a price curve), saving, and offline progress—earning even while the game is closed.

This continues the shared foundation. Each step adds one genre piece on top of the LEVEL 01 state boundary. The hero is the original Ebi Tenjiroh (海老・天次郎).
