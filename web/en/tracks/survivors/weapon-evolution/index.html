DEEP DIVE / DATA-DRIVEN WEAPONS

Does evolution mean
rewriting the shooter code?

No. You swap weapon data. The firing loop stays the same while cooldown and count change the feel and power.

1

Weapon struct

Keep name and numbers together.

type weapon struct{ cooldown, speed, count, damage int }
2

Conditions

Track keys like kills and item pickup with flags.

if kills >= 20 && itemTaken
3

Swap

Assign a new weapon{...} and evolution is done.

weapon = weapon{"Ebi Storm", 16, 9, 3, 2}
