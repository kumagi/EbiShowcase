GO + EBITENGINE

Filter candidates by arrival time

danger maps a cell to the number of frames before its blast. The search considers waiting as well as movement in four directions.

arrival := nextStep * moveEvery
blastAt, threatened := danger[next]
safe := !threatened ||
    arrival+8 < blastAt ||
    arrival > blastAt+blastLife
if safe {
    queue = append(queue, nextNode)
}

WHY IT WORKS

A route in time beats nearest distance

An AI that only seeks a nearby cell can wait in a blast at a corner. Adding future time lets it choose a longer route that clears danger before detonation.

CHALLENGE

Change its caution

Increase the 8 in arrival+8 so the AI avoids danger sooner. Then explain why a margin that is too large can leave it with no route.
