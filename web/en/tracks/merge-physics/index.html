Merge Physics Puzzle

Build a Suika Game-style puzzle where matching fruit merge into a bigger one when they touch. Start with falling circles, then add gravity, collision, pushing overlapping circles apart until they don't overlap (penetration correction), bounce, and merging.

This continues the shared foundation. Each step adds one genre piece on top of the LEVEL 01 state boundary. The hero is the original Ebi Tenjiroh (海老・天次郎).
