PLAY FIRST

Cast One Ray per Screen Column

Move and turn with arrows/WASD or the on-screen buttons. In the final step, shoot with Space or RAY.

DEEP DIVE

Project 2D distances into a 3D-looking view

The left column uses a left-facing ray, the center uses the forward ray, and the right uses a right-facing ray. Compare the minimap rays with the FPS columns: the actual world is still 2D.
