DEEP DIVE / ICE

Ice is
a two-act play

Act 1 (forming): pull frost inward and grow an ice-PNG core. Act 2 (shatter): hide the crystal, fling ice sprites outward, add additive sparkles and a bloom ring. A tiny phase/timer state machine puts a story on the same particle engine.

Switch acts with phase

forming → shatter. Same Particle type, different jobs.

// Act 1: pull frost inward
f.x += (castX - f.x) * 0.06
// Act 2: shatter shards outward
spawn(IcePNG, vx: cos(a)*sp, grav: 0.12)
spawn(Ring) // bloom on break

WHY TWO ACTS?

Build-up sells the break

Shattering after a beat feels more magical than an instant burst.

WHY ICE PNG?

Shape reads as element

Round sparks don’t say ice—sharp textures do half the work.
