Why does spinning
fling it away?

Transforms are matrix multiplications applied in the order you write them. Rotate while the top-left is the origin and the image swings in a big arc. Move the center to (-w/2,-h/2) first, rotate, then Translate to its place, and it spins in the middle.

IN THE REAL GAME

Spin about the center

To-origin → rotate/scale → place. That order is the whole trick.

op := &ebiten.DrawImageOptions{}
op.GeoM.Translate(-w/2, -h/2) // 中心を原点へ
op.GeoM.Scale(1.7, 1.7)
op.GeoM.Rotate(0.7)           // ラジアンで回す
op.GeoM.Translate(cx, cy)     // 置きたい場所へ
screen.DrawImage(sprite, op)
