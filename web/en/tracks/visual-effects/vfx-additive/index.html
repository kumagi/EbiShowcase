Calculate additive color

Red plus green makes yellow; all three make white

Additive blending adds light colors. Red (1,0,0) plus green (0,1,0) becomes (1,1,0), yellow. Add blue too and the result is white.

red + green = (1,0,0) + (0,1,0)
          = (1,1,0) = yellow
red + green + blue = white

Why is stacked
light white?
