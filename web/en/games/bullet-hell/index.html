HOW IT WORKS

Divide angles and turn them into velocity

Divide a full circle by the number of shots. Cosine gives horizontal velocity and sine gives vertical velocity. Changing the starting angle for every volley makes the pattern appear to rotate.

angle := spin + i*2*math.Pi/count
vx := math.Cos(angle) * speed
vy := math.Sin(angle) * speed
