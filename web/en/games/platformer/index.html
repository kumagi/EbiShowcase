HOW IT WORKS

Resolve horizontal and vertical movement separately

Move along X and push the player out of overlapping walls, then do the same along Y for floors. Subtracting the camera position while drawing makes the world scroll.

player.X += vx
resolveHorizontal()
player.Y += vy
resolveVertical()
screenX := player.X - cameraX

GAME + LESSON

The base of a Mario-like action game

01

Accelerate

Add horizontal input to velocity and gravity to vertical velocity each frame.

02

Push out

Move out of overlapping terrain in the opposite direction to stop at floors and walls.

03

Show the world

Use a following camera to render a course wider than the screen.
