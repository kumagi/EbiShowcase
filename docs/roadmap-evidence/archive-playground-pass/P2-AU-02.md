## Changes

- Added `audiolab.OneShot`, a dependency-free float32 PCM synthesizer with sine, square, and deterministic noise wave choices, frequency, and exponential decay.
- Added `waveform-se`, a playable WASM lab that cycles the three original one-shot sound families with tap, pointer, or Space and displays a matching waveform reaction.
- The audio is synthesized at runtime; no third-party sample or music asset is used.
