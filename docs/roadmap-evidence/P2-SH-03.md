## Changes

- Added `distort.kage` plus the safe `Distort` helper. It shifts UV before `imageSrc0At`, selecting water, heat haze, or an expanding impact ring entirely through uniforms.
- Added the playable `uv-distortion` demo. Tap, mouse, or Space cycles all three effects and resets the impact wave; fallback drawing remains available when the shader cannot compile.
- Extended both Shader Lab hubs with the third bilingual play panel and a concise explanation of the shared coordinate-sampling idea.
